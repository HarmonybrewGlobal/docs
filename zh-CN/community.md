## Code of Conduct

We encourage technical discussions, but strictly prohibit the posting of non-technical, off-topic comments in community channels (issues, PRs, discussion forums, etc.), including but not limited to debates about the merits of different operating systems, personal attacks, or political topics. The maintenance team reserves the right to close or delete relevant interactions without prior notice.
